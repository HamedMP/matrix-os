# ACT 3: THE FLOW -- Image Generation Prompts

Visual language: Warm naturals, golden hour, earth tones, amber screen glow (never blue). Spike Jonze "Her" color grading. Wider compositions, steady camera, intimate but spacious. Every location feels lived-in and authentic.

---

### Shot 3.1 -- Voice Creates (The First Words)

**Prompt**: Over-the-shoulder medium shot of a young Scandinavian man sitting at a minimal wooden desk in a Stockholm apartment, morning golden hour light streaming through sheer linen curtains, warm sunlight casting long soft shadows across the room, he speaks toward a monitor displaying an elegant dark UI with a glowing amber waveform responding to his voice, plants on the windowsill, warm wood floors, muted earth-tone walls, shallow depth of field, shot on Arri Alexa with Cooke S4 50mm lens, Spike Jonze "Her" color grading, warm amber highlights, soft film grain, cinematic photorealistic, 16:9 aspect ratio --ar 16:9

**Negative prompt**: Blue light, cold tones, sterile office, corporate environment, multiple monitors, cable clutter, dark room, neon, sci-fi, futuristic hologram, text overlay, watermark

**Style notes**: The monitor glow should be warm amber/gold, not the typical cool blue of tech products. The room should feel like a real person lives here -- not an IKEA catalog, but a comfortable creative space with personality. The waveform on screen is subtle, not flashy.

### Shot 3.15 -- Federation Map

**Prompt**: A stylized dark globe floating in black space, softly glowing warm amber and gold nodes marking cities across the world -- Mexico City, Cairo, Rajasthan, Shanghai, Stockholm, Sao Paulo, Lagos, Tokyo, Nairobi, and more -- thin organic lines of warm light connecting the nodes like a living constellation, the connections pulse gently with soft particle effects, the globe has subtle topographic detail but is mostly dark with the nodes providing the light, the overall effect is organic and alive not corporate or technical, like bioluminescent organisms connected by neural pathways, dark background with subtle warm ambient glow, wide shot centered on the globe, cinematic, 16:9 aspect ratio --ar 16:9

**Negative prompt**: Blue network diagram, corporate infographic, neon colors, grid lines, data visualization, cold tones, flat design, UI elements, text labels, country borders highlighted, political map, tech company aesthetic, hologram

**Style notes**: This should feel more like a living organism or constellation than a network diagram. The nodes should pulse gently at different rates, like heartbeats. The connecting lines should be organic and slightly curved, not straight geometric lines. The warm amber glow of each node should softly illuminate the nearby geography. The overall impression is of a quiet, decentralized, breathing network -- owned by no one, connecting everyone.
